About Us

We are actively involved in Mining and Extraction of Raw Quartz, Quartzite, Mica and Feldspar over an extent of 17.691 Hectares/43.69 Acres in Survey No:373 of Mamuduru Village, Chejarla Mandal, SPSR Nellore District, India.

We are licenced to extract and supply 98,788 Mts/Year and the same can be stepped up to 2,00,000 M.tons/Year.
We also process and Manufacture “B” Grade Quartz lumps of different sizes from 40 mm to 1800mm apart from boulders of 2 to 3 Ft size extracted from Mine.

We manufacture Grit in seizes -80/120,16/24,24/60,30/60 30/80 ,8/16 and 4/8 and Quartz Powder in 200,300,325 and 400 mesh for use in manufacturing of different industries.
We can arrange to supply the above products in 50 KG PP bags and Jumbo bags of 1 to 1.50 M.Tons capacity as per Customer Requirements
